StenTech Opens 22nd Location in Phoenix

${configuration.authorName!'Jennifer Martinez'} ${configuration.authorTitle!'HR Manager'}

We're thrilled to announce the grand opening of our 22nd manufacturing location in Phoenix, Arizona. This state-of-the-art facility represents a significant investment in our commitment to serving customers across the southwestern United States with industry-leading turnaround times and quality.

Expanding Our Manufacturing Capabilities

The new 45,000 square foot Phoenix facility features the latest in SMT stencil manufacturing technology, including:

  • Advanced laser cutting systems for precision aperture creation
  • Automated quality inspection equipment
  • Climate-controlled production environment
  • Dedicated design studio with 8 CAD workstations
  • Same-day and next-day production capabilities

Creating Local Jobs

The Phoenix location will create 35 new jobs in the local community, including positions for production technicians, CAD designers, quality inspectors, and customer service representatives. We're actively recruiting talented individuals who share our commitment to manufacturing excellence.

"Opening our Phoenix facility allows us to better serve our growing customer base in Arizona, Nevada, and southern California with the fastest turnaround times in the industry. This investment demonstrates StenTech's commitment to being where our customers need us." — Tom Richards, VP of Operations

Grand Opening Celebration

Join us for our grand opening celebration on November 20th from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM. The event will include facility tours, equipment demonstrations, and networking opportunities. RSVP to events@electronic.com by November 15th.

For more information about the Phoenix location, contact our regional manager at (602) 555-0123 or visit our locations page.
